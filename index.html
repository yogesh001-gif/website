Common Illnesses

Fever

Symptoms:

  • High temperature
  • Chills and sweating
  • Headache
  • Muscle aches

Common Treatments:

  • Paracetamol
  • Ibuprofen
  • Rest and hydration

Common Cold

Symptoms:

  • Runny nose
  • Sore throat
  • Cough
  • Congestion

Common Treatments:

  • Antihistamines
  • Decongestants
  • Cough suppressants

Headache

Symptoms:

  • Pain in head/neck
  • Sensitivity to light
  • Nausea

Common Treatments:

  • Aspirin
  • Paracetamol
  • Rest in dark room

Common Medicines Guide

Paracetamol

Uses: Pain relief, fever reduction

Adult dosage: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours

Max 4000mg per day

Ibuprofen

Uses: Pain relief, inflammation

Adult dosage: 200-400mg every 4-6 hours

Take with food

Antihistamines

Uses: Allergies, cold symptoms

Follow package instructions

May cause drowsiness

Emergency Information

When to Seek Emergency Care

  • सांस लेने में तकलीफ (Difficulty breathing)
  • छाती में दर्द (Chest pain)
  • पेट में तेज दर्द (Severe abdominal pain)
  • तेज बुखार (High fever)
  • सिर में चोट (Head injury)
